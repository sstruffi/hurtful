Hurtful

A conceptual video exploring the emotion of hurt through symbolism, repetition, and visual breakdown.

Concept Film Visual Storytelling Symbolism Direction / Editing
Back to Videography Watch on YouTube

Overview

This project explores the emotion of hurt through the visual metaphor of a clock slowly breaking apart. The clock represents time, repetition, and the feeling of being trapped in an emotional cycle.

I developed the concept, shaped the visual direction, and used editing and symbolism to communicate emotional tension without relying on dialogue.

My Role

I was responsible for the creative direction of the piece, including the concept, visual choices, symbolism, and final edit. My goal was to make the emotion feel clear through color, repetition, and the breakdown of the clock over time.

Visual Approach

The project uses strong contrast to communicate emotion. Red appears throughout the clock and character to suggest pain and urgency, while darker tones and broken textures create a feeling of vulnerability and isolation.

The final shift to blue suggests release and movement beyond the emotion of hurt.

Process and Learning

This project helped me think more carefully about how symbolism and visual hierarchy shape meaning. I focused on making each design decision support the central idea, rather than adding details that did not strengthen the concept.

It reflects my interest in creative work that combines narrative, design, and emotional impact.

Outcome

This piece demonstrates my ability to communicate complex emotion through visual storytelling, symbolism, and strong creative direction.
