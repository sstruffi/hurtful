Hurtful

A conceptual video exploring the emotion of hurt through symbolism, repetition, and visual breakdown.

Concept Film Visual Storytelling Symbolism

Concept

This project explores the emotion "hurtful" through the visual metaphor of a clock slowly breaking apart. The clock represents time slipping away and the feeling of being stuck in a repeating emotional cycle. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

As the video progresses, the clock deteriorates, symbolizing loss, vulnerability, and emotional damage. By the end, the breakdown leads to a shift, representing release and the possibility of moving forward. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

Visual Approach

The project uses strong contrast to communicate emotion. Red is used throughout the clock and character to symbolize pain and urgency, contrasted against white and darker tones to create a sense of vulnerability and isolation. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

The exposed gears and cogs represent internal fragility, while broken glass and sharp textures emphasize the emotional intensity of the piece. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

Narrative Elements

The main character mirrors the clock by wearing red, showing they are trapped within the same cycle. Four additional figures represent divisions of the clock, reinforcing isolation and emotional impact.

The final shift to blue symbolizes breaking free from the cycle and moving beyond the emotion of hurt. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}

Outcome

This project demonstrates my ability to communicate complex emotion through visual storytelling, symbolism, and design principles like contrast and texture.

It reflects the type of creative work I want to continue exploring—projects that combine narrative, visual design, and emotional impact.
